Bucket List

1. Watch an NBA Game in Person Live

     As a passionate basketball fan, watching an NBA game live is a once-in-a-lifetime experience I dream of. Growing up, I spent countless hours playing basketball and following my favorite teams and players. Witnessing the intensity and energy of an NBA match from the stands, surrounded by thousands of enthusiastic fans, would be unforgettable. I want to feel the excitement of every buzzer-beater, dunk, and three-pointer in real time. It’s not just about watching a game — it’s about immersing myself in the sport I’ve loved since childhood. This experience would fulfill a lifelong passion and serve as a reminder to enjoy life’s memorable moments.

2. Travel Abroad

     Traveling abroad is a dream that I am determined to accomplish. Exploring different countries will allow me to immerse myself in new cultures, try diverse cuisines, and experience the beauty of the world firsthand. Whether it's visiting iconic landmarks, strolling through vibrant cities, or relaxing in serene landscapes, every journey will offer me a fresh perspective and unforgettable memories. I also see traveling as a way to step out of my comfort zone, meet people from different backgrounds, and broaden my understanding of the world. From tasting authentic dishes to learning new languages, each experience will enrich my life and fuel my personal growth. Traveling internationally will also serve as a reward for my hard work and a reminder to enjoy the moments life has to offer. It's a goal that represents both adventure and self-discovery, making it an essential part of my bucket list.

3. Buy My Own Car

     Owning my own car is another goal that symbolizes financial independence and personal achievement. After years of enduring long and exhausting commutes from Bulacan to Manila, having my own vehicle would significantly improve my quality of life. A car would provide convenience, flexibility, and more time for studying, relaxing, or pursuing hobbies. Beyond its practicality, purchasing a car with my hard-earned money would also be a reflection of my perseverance and dedication. It’s a milestone that represents the rewards of my professional efforts and a step closer to the independence I strive for.

4. Graduate with a Bachelor's Degree in Information Technology

     Earning my degree in Information Technology from the University of the East - Manila is a significant milestone I aspire to achieve. It represents the culmination of years of hard work, sacrifice, and dedication. This accomplishment would not only validate my academic efforts but also open up countless opportunities in the tech industry. I see my degree as a stepping stone toward a stable career, a way to gain financial independence, and a means to support my family. Graduation will also serve as a symbol of resilience, knowing that I overcame challenges like long commutes, academic struggles, and time management issues to reach this point. It will be a proud moment for both me and my loved ones.

5. Land a Job in the IT Industry

     After completing my degree, I am determined to secure a job in the IT field. Working in a professional environment will allow me to apply the technical knowledge and practical skills I’ve gained throughout college. I’m excited to contribute to projects that solve real-world problems while further developing my expertise. Starting my career in a reputable company would also provide mentorship opportunities and expose me to the latest technologies and industry practices. Landing an IT job will mark the beginning of my professional journey, offering the financial stability needed to support myself and my family. It will also be a stepping stone toward achieving greater career milestones in the future.

6. Develop My Own Software or Application

     One of my biggest goals is to create my own software or mobile application. I am passionate about using technology to simplify tasks, solve problems, or even entertain users. Whether it’s a productivity app, an educational platform, or a game, developing my own software will allow me to express my creativity and innovation. Seeing my project come to life, knowing it has the potential to impact others positively, is a dream I’m eager to pursue. Additionally, this achievement would be a testament to my growth as a developer and could even open opportunities for entrepreneurial ventures or collaborations with other tech professionals.

7. Start My Own Business

     Entrepreneurship has always intrigued me, and starting my own business is a long-term aspiration. Combining my passion for technology with my desire to solve problems, I hope to establish a company that offers innovative digital solutions. Whether it’s a tech startup, an app development service, or an e-commerce platform, I want to create something meaningful and impactful. Being my own boss would allow me to manage projects, make decisions, and lead a team of like-minded individuals. While it’s undoubtedly a challenging goal, I’m eager to embrace the risks and rewards that come with building a business from the ground up. Achieving this would not only provide financial growth but also leave a lasting legacy in the tech industry.

8. Buy/Build My Own House

     Owning a house is a significant life goal that symbolizes stability and independence. I aspire to have a comfortable space I can call my own, designed to reflect my personality and preferences. Providing a secure and welcoming home for myself and my family is something I deeply value. It’s not just about the property itself but the sense of achievement that comes with it. Buying a house would mark a major financial milestone and be a testament to my dedication and hard work.

9. Go on a Cruise

     Sailing across the ocean on a luxurious cruise is a dream vacation I hope to experience. With endless views of the sea, exciting shore excursions, and onboard entertainment, a cruise offers both relaxation and adventure. It’s a perfect way to explore multiple destinations while enjoying all the comforts of a floating resort

10. Meet My Favorite Celebrities

     Meeting one of my favorite celebrities or basketball players would be an unforgettable experience. Whether it’s having a conversation with my celebrity crush Emma Stone, whose acting talent and charm I admire, or shaking hands with Stephen Curry or LeBron James, two basketball legends I’ve looked up to for years, it would be a dream come true. I’d also love the opportunity to meet Andy Samberg, whose comedic brilliance has brought me countless laughs, or Justin Timberlake, whose music and performances have always been inspiring. Even a brief moment with any of these icons would be a memory I’d treasure forever.
